Egg Sandwich
Recipe!

A delicious and easy to make egg sandwich featuring fluffy scrambled or fried eggs, nestled between toasted bread with a touch of butter, cheese, and fresh veggies. Perfect for a quick breakfast or snack!

  • Bread Slices
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese Slice
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Toast Bread - Spread butter on bread and toast until golden.
  2. Cook eggs - Fry or scramble eggs with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble - Place eggs on one slice, add cheese and optional toppings.
  4. Finish - Cover with the other slice, press lightly, and enjoy!
